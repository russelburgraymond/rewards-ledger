Graphs

The Graphs page gives you a visual view of trends so you can see profit direction, compare income vs expense periods, and spot which categories are driving totals.

What the first graph page includes
  • Net Profit Over Time line chart
  • Income vs Expense bar chart with Combined Totals and Stacked by App views
  • Category Breakdown donut chart
  • Summary totals for income, expense, and net profit
Filters

The Graphs page includes filters so you can narrow the chart data without changing the ledger.

  • Date mode: All Time, Current Year, or Custom Range
  • Group by: Day, Week, or Month
  • Income / Expense View: Combined Totals or Stacked by App
  • Apps, Categories, and Assets multi-select filters

If you choose Current Year, the charts only use entries dated from January 1 of the current year forward.

How profit is calculated

The Graphs page uses category behavior types so the visuals follow the same logic as the rest of the app.

  • Income-style behaviors are counted as positive
  • Expense, Investment, and similar outflow behaviors are counted as negative for profit
  • Transfer, Neutral, and Adjustment are excluded from profit math
Income vs Expense stacked by app

The Income vs Expense chart can now split each bar into app sections so you can see how much of the total came from each app.

  • Stacked by App shows each period as stacked sections with a legend
  • Each stacked bar pair is labeled directly underneath as Income and Expense so you can tell the two bar types apart without looking elsewhere
  • If you manually select apps in the filter, the stacked chart legend keeps those selected apps visible so the color key stays consistent
  • Combined Totals keeps the simpler side-by-side bars

This is useful when you want to compare overall income and expense totals without losing visibility into which app contributed each part.

Notes

The charts are self-contained and do not rely on outside script links or outside image links.

This keeps RewardLedger portable and in line with the rest of the app.
